# Conference Venue

San Diego Supercomputer Center (SDSC)<br>
University of California, San Diego<br>
SDSC Auditorium<br>
9836 Hopkins Dr, La Jolla, CA 92037<br>
[Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/place/32%C2%B053'04.0%22N+117%C2%B014'20.9%22W/@32.884443,-117.2413197,17z)<br>
[Visitor and parking information](https://www.sdsc.edu/contact.html)

The SDSC Auditorium is located on the ground floor of SDSC's east entrance, just off the driveway on Hopkins Dr, close to the Hopkins Parking Structure, at the northwest end of the UC San Diego campus.

## Parking

Parking permits are required to park on the UC San Diego campus. Non-UC visitors can purchase permits at a reasonable rate. The most convenient parking is the **Hopkins Parking Structure**, located on Hopkins Dr and Voigt Dr, just south of SDSC.

More details: [UC San Diego visitor parking](http://transportation.ucsd.edu/parking/visitor/conference.html)

## Getting to SDSC

**Airport**: San Diego International Airport (SAN) is the closest airport to UC San Diego and SDSC.

**Rideshare**: Use Lyft or Uber with "San Diego Supercomputer Center" as the destination.

**Drop-off point**: 9836 Hopkins Dr, La Jolla, CA 92037

**Public transit**: [UC San Diego transit options](https://transportation.ucsd.edu/alternatives/transit/)
